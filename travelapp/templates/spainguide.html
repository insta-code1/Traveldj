Andalucia Gourmet Discovery Tour

Imagine being picked up at your collection point, by your personal driver in one of our exclusive new 7 person Mercedes tourer and being whisked away to our first stop, UNESCO World Heritage Granada. Enjoy some of the most magnificent countryside passing through unique pueblo blanco, white villages, that appear to have stood still for over 2,000 years. Avoiding the queues in Granada, you will have a guided tour of the magnificent Alhambra Palace and Gardens, the home of Moorish Sultans from the 12th century, virtually unchanged today. Marvel at the mosaics, the scale, the sheer opulence, the scents from the garden’s, the views. A sensorial experience. Then on for our first tastings at one of Andalucia’s most loved wineries. Delight in plates of homemade tapas, from ancient recipes, with a glass of vino out on the terrace. Perhaps you will be joined by the owners over lunch. This afternoon we visit 2 boutique wineries, who’s wines have won international acclaim. Savour the different flavours, learn about the terroir that make these wines unique, enjoy the stories maybe even crush a grape or two!

Stop for sundowners en-route to your handpicked accommodation, perhaps tonight it will be a 15th century Monastery, an Olive Mill, or a beautifully restored Roman Townhouse. No hotel chains here! Wherever it is you can be rest assured that we chose it for it’s authentic flavour, with of course all modern luxuries.

That night, wander into the village, enjoy a glass of vino or two, maybe some tapas overlooking the village square or perhaps you’ll try the local gourmet dishes in our top recommended restaurants. Finish off with a late glass of sherry maybe listening to local musicians while taking in the views.

And so it goes for the next four days. You will be picked up by your driver mid morning, after you have had a chance to take in a coffee in the village square and experience village life in a real Andalucian pueblo blanco, where the surrounds have remained pretty much unchanged since Moorish times. Have a walk through the narrow cobbled streets, stop and chat with a local. Enjoy a slower pace of life. Perhaps today we will visit a Roman Amphitheatre and see the site where the earliest wines were produced for the Roman emporers.

Andalucia is blessed with the freshest of seafood, produce unique to the area, rich local wines that speak of the terroir combined with Michelin standard culinary skills and all uniquely savoured against an ancient Moorish backdrop, that make for a truly outstanding experience.

Or experience life in Moorish times with a walking and tapas tour taking in the highlights of this UNESCO heritage city of Granada.
